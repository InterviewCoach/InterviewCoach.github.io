The App Features

  • Sign up & Log in

    Users can create their own account, which they can then log in and out of as they choose.

  • Practice Interviewing

    Users can create a mock interview session.

  • Thoughtful Questions

    Durring the mock interview session, a user will be prompted with real interview questions. Their response will be recorded and analyzed.

  • Report

    After each session, a user can see a report visualizing their use of common filler words.

  • History

    Users can see clear data visualizations of all their past sessions.

  • Navigate

    Side drawer navigation allows for easy access across our app.

  • About

    Our friendly about page allows users to get more information about the functionality of our app.
